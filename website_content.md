Are you aspiring to become a licensed pharmacy technician in Canada? Are you feeling overwhelmed by the daunting prospect of the PEBC OSCE (Objective Structured Clinical Examination) or need practice to develop the habits necessary for effective pharmacy technician-client interactions? Description
Who are you?
Have you recently graduated from a pharmacy technician program at a Canadian college? Are you currently preparing for the transition to professional practice? Our course offers supplementary support and exam preparation resources to complement your academic curriculum, ensuring that you are well-prepared to ace the PEBC OSCE exam and to excel in the workforce.
What Would You Learn?
Our curriculum covers a wide range of topics, including:
Communication Skills: Effective communication is vital for providing excellent patient care and collaborating with other healthcare professionals. We focus on enhancing your verbal and non-verbal communication skills, enabling you to confidently interact with patients, healthcare teams, and other stakeholders.
Knowledge application: You will learn how to apply your knowledge and pharmaceutical principles to real-world scenarios, enhancing your problem- solving skills to enable you make informed decisions and provide optimal patient care.
Case Analysis and Problem-Solving: The PEBC OSCE exam includes case-based scenarios that require critical thinking and problem-solving skills. We will guide you through various case studies, helping you develop a systematic approach to analyze and solve complex pharmaceutical cases.
Professionalism and Ethics: As a pharmacy technician, maintaining high ethical standards and professionalism is essential. Our course emphasizes the importance of ethical decision-making, patient confidentiality, and professional conduct, ensuring you are well-prepared for the exam and your future career.
Why Choose Us?
We provide comprehensive training and support to help you excel in the PEBC OSCE exam and equip you with the skills necessary to succeed as a pharmacy technician.
Expert Guidance: Our coaching team comprises experienced pharmacists and pharmacy technicians who have successfully navigated the PEBC OSCE exam themselves. Benefit from their wealth of knowledge and firsthand insights as they guide you through the intricacies of the examination process.
Structured Curriculum: Our course is meticulously designed to cover all essential aspects of the PEBC OSCE exam. Our curriculum ensures comprehensive preparation across all competency areas.
Interactive Learning: Engage in interactive learning experiences that mirror the format and rigor of the PEBC OSCE exam. Participate in mock OSCE sessions, role-playing scenarios, and practice questions designed to simulate real-world pharmacy practice settings.
Personalized Support: Receive personalized attention and support throughout your preparation journey. Our instructors provide constructive feedback, identify areas for improvement, and offer tailored strategies to address your individual learning needs.
Effective Study Strategies: Learn proven study techniques and time-management strategies to optimize your preparation efforts. We equip you with the tools and resources needed to stay focused, organized, and confident as you work towards achieving your goal.
Peer Collaboration: Connect with fellow students preparing for the PEBC OSCE exam and foster a supportive learning community. Engage in robust discussions, share experiences, exchange study tips, and collaborate on practice sessions to enhance your preparation together.

Method of delivery
Online delivery consisting of a combination of asynchronous self-paced learning, group practice sessions and weekly synchronous online live classes where we meticulously go through over 180 cases. Each student will have a chance to practice cases weekly, using our framework, in order to develop a mental memory of the correct way to approach solving cases during the real exam. Real time feedback is provided to help identify areas of improvement.
